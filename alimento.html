Live food offers a nutritious and stimulating enrichment for ornamental fish, with options catering to various fish sizes and preferences. Tiny brine shrimp nauplii are perfect for fish fry and smaller species, while bloodworms and daphnia provide a protein-rich treat for many fish. Microworms and vinegar eels are excellent choices for in-between sized fish, while even earthworms can be chopped for larger tank inhabitants. However, live food can introduce parasites or cloud the water, so responsible sourcing and careful feeding are essential.
